# AI-Assisted Remediation

## Purpose

AI-assisted remediation uses models or decision support to recommend or trigger safer recovery actions.

## Use Cases

- incident classification support
- response recommendation
- remediation prioritization
- alert aggregation

## Design Guidance

- explain the reasoning behind recommendations
- keep human approval for high-risk actions
- validate AI suggestions with operational evidence

## Evidence To Collect

- recommendation output
- accuracy notes
- override records
- validation results

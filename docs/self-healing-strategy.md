# Self-Healing Strategy

## Purpose

The strategy defines how the organization will use automation to shorten detection, remediation, and recovery time.

## Strategy Themes

- automate low-risk recovery actions first
- keep strong guardrails in place
- validate every automated action
- learn from failures and overrides
- measure business impact, not just technical speed

## Strategy Questions

- which failure patterns are safe to automate?
- what approval rules are needed?
- how will automation outcomes be measured?
- what happens when the automation fails?

## Strategy Outputs

- automation priorities
- guardrail design
- validation criteria
- improvement roadmap
